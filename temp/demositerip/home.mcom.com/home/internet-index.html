Internet Directory

This is your Internet Directory. Actually, this is more like a directory of directories, as there are many directories -- organized a variety of different ways -- on the Internet.

Subject Directories

These directories are organized by subject matter, such as "Current Affairs," "Entertainment," and "Government and Politics."

The Whole Internet Catalog
By O'Reilly and Associates, this is one of the most complete subject catalogs available.

EINet Galaxy
This is a large subject directory that also allows you to search for subjects you may be interested in.

Yahoo
Stanford University offers a directory of Internet services that features over 13,000 entries. Yahoo also offers listings of the most popular pages, the ability to search the directory of services, and (if you're up for adventure) a way to visit a random page.

Type of Service
The World-Wide Web Virtual Library offers a broad range of topics searchable by subject as well as service type.

Best of the Web '94
This directory is a collection its voters call the best Internet services available in 1994.

Commercial Directories

These directories organize commercial servers and services on the Internet. Such commercial servers may contain information about a company and its products, and even easy ways to place orders.

Commercial Services on the Net
Open Market, Inc. offers this large alphabetical listing of corporate Internet servers. You can also search the list.

Interesting Business Sites
Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute offers a listing of 50+ sites.

Internet Business Directory
This is the store front for 200+ companies that are offering product information on the Internet.

CommerceNet
This directory offers business listings and corporate home pages for a major Northern California electronic commerce initiative.

MecklerWeb
This directory contains pointers to lots of different companies in lots of different fields.
