Reporting a Bug?

If you think you've found a bug, we'd appreciate your help to track down the problem you're reporting. This will help us fix the bug as quickly as possible.

Make sure your feedback includes a summary of the problem: what happened in as much detail as possible, what we should do to make it happen again, what were you expecting. Anything that you can tell us to help us reproduce this bug will help.
